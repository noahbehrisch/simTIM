For the camera ready to appear in the proceedings the instructions below are to be adhered to:

- Your paper needs to be revised according to the comments provided by the reviewers that are attached to the notification.

- Your camera ready version must conform to the instructions that will be provided by the Proceedings chair (Buse.Atli@liu.se) including adherence to the guidelines for use of AI, and copyright transfer.

- The given page limits are strict (up to 11 pages) with no option to buy additional pages.

- There should be a subtitle “Short paper” in the revised version.

- The list of authors (names, emails, affiliations, order) and the title of the paper are not allowed to be changed after notification. If a correction is needed (e.g., because the author name was misspelled), we need to approve the change.

Please further note that at least one author must register and present the paper at the conference for the paper to be included in the proceedings.

Note also that the deadline for the booking of a hotel room at the block rate is 24th May (see the web for details).
